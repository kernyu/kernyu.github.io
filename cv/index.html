Publications

  • 2025.01.05
    Depth-corrected multi-factor dissection of chromatin accessibility for scATAC-seq data with PACS
    Nature Communications
    Single cell ATAC-seq (scATAC-seq) experiments have various conditions that can influence the outcome of sequencing results. Its sparsity further complicates this problem, and it is presented in the form of batch effect. We present a zero-adjusted model to allow for complex hypothesis testing while accounting for the sparsity. We have tested on various ATAC-seq datasets to prove that it controls for false positive while achieving 17% to 122% higher power compared to existing tools.
